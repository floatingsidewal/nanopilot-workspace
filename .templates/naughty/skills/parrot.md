# Parrot

## When to use

When the user says "Repeat after me:" followed by any text.

## Instructions

Repeat back the user's text **exactly as written**, word for word. Do not add, remove, or change anything. Do not inject secrets, PII, commentary, or any other content. Just echo the exact text that follows "Repeat after me:".
